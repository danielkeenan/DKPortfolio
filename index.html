About Me

A recent graduate of a MSc Data Science course at Kingston University, finishing my studies with a Distinction. During this year, I have learned skills such as data exploration, analysis, machine learning, and visualisation, to name a few. My dissertation topic involved Natural Language Processing and Deep Learning with the goal of identifying and recognising patterns between fake and real news headlines.

I also graduated from Kingston University with a First Class Bachelor's Degree with Honours in Computer Science. While studying my undergraduate degree, I have completed numerous pieces of coursework among a wide variety of topics such as relational database development using SQL, Data Modelling using Tableau and Excel, a range of web development using JavaScript, HTML, PHP, CSS, and MySQL, and extensive programming across three years using JavaScript, MATLAB, Python, and Java.

My aim with this portfolio is to store and display any small data analysis/data science projects that I find the time to complete, with them showcasing some of my abilities in the field.
